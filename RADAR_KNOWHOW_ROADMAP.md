# RADAR Know How — Internal Roadmap Note

Last update: 7 April 2026
Status: Internal content roadmap

## 1. Purpose

Give `Know How` a clearer long-term structure so beginner content grows like a path, not like a pile.

This note is for:
- content planning
- sequence decisions
- keeping beginner guidance coherent

This note is not:
- final IA
- page design
- public-facing copy

## 2. Core Direction

`Know How` should feel like:
- a smart path for someone who is new
- a calm upgrade from zero chaos to real orientation
- young-reader-friendly without talking down to people

Tone target:
- human
- clear
- slightly funny when useful
- respectful
- no finance-bro energy
- no school textbook energy

This is the current beginner path in order:

## 4. Next Logical Topics After Current Path

These should follow after the current beginner path.
Goal: move from “I understand the basics” to “I can make calmer real decisions.”

### Rozumím základům → další praktická vrstva
1. `Psychologie peněz — proč neděláme průšvihy hlavou, ale emocemi`
Why:
- natural next step after ETF / DCA / simple start
- explains why knowing the right thing and doing the right thing are not the same

2. `Kolik si nechat bokem a kolik už může jít do investic`
Why:
- practical bridge between reserve and investing
- removes one of the most common beginner questions

3. `První broker bez chaosu — co fakt řešit a co už ne`
Why:
- useful right after “jak začít investovat”
- should simplify choice, not turn into comparison paralysis

### Jdu víc do hloubky
4. `Kdy dává smysl jedna akcie — a kdy je to zatím zbytečné drama`
Why:
- logical step after ETF foundation
- introduces stock-picking without pushing it too early

5. `Jak poznat, že je investice spíš hype než plán`
Why:
- useful for young readers exposed to TikTok/IG finance noise
- helps with scam resistance and judgment

6. `Auto v 18: svoboda, nebo nejdražší chyba?`
Why:
- extremely relevant for young readers
- broad money decision, not just “cars content”
- high click potential without losing educational value

7. `Psychologie prvního většího nákupu — proč člověk utratí víc, když už “si to zaslouží” `
Why:
- practical money behavior topic
- naturally extends from budgeting, risk, and long-term thinking

### Víc do života a větších rozhodnutí
8. `Hypotéka bez iluzí pro mladé`
Why:
- strong bridge from beginner investing to real adult money decisions
- aspirational, practical, and highly relevant

9. `Co banka řeší dřív, než ti půjčí na bydlení`
Why:
- clean companion piece to the mortgage topic
- useful even for people who are not buying yet

10. `Kdy má smysl řešit bydlení a kdy je lepší zatím budovat flexibilitu`
Why:
- balances the mortgage conversation
- keeps Radar from sounding like “kup byt co nejdřív”

11. `První výplata bez chaosu — co udělat dřív, než zmizí`
Why:
- perfect entry for younger readers moving from school/brigády to work
- practical and shareable

12. `Co tě stojí nejvíc peněz, i když to nevypadá nebezpečně`
Why:
- broad beginner-friendly topic
- can cover fees, lifestyle creep, bad debt, random consumption traps

## 8. Content Rules For This Roadmap

Every future `Know How` piece should ideally do at least one of these:
- remove beginner confusion
- lower emotional chaos
- improve decision quality
- connect money to real life choices

Avoid:
- writing product-first content too early
- adding advanced finance terms just to sound smart
- drifting into generic motivation content
- repeating the same ETF beginner article in new clothes

## 9. Decision Summary

- Current beginner path is strong enough to act as the real onboarding ladder
- The next best layer is not “harder finance,” but calmer real-life decision content
- `Psychologie peněz` is the best next bridge topic
- `Auto v 18` and mortgage/bank topics are strong because they connect finance to real young-reader choices
